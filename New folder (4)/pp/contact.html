CONTACT

1. Sign the Qatar Internet Exchange (QIX) Policy.

2. Fill out the below Form (Membership Application Form)

3. Submit the Membership Application Form, along with the signed QIX policy, to the QIX Steering Committee by email qixinfo@qix.qa

4. Once approved, the applicant will receive a confirmation email.

5. The QIX Technical Team will then contact the applicant to cooperate and implement the QIX technical changes required for the connectivity.

6. Once the connection is established, the applicant status will change to member and the peering can be initiated with other QIX members.
